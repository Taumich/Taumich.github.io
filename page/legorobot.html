1:st place in KTH EV3 Sumo Robot Championship

Using my previous experience in autonomous mini-sumo robot competitions, and with the help of my team (Group 4), a highly competative lego-sumo robot was built.

The competition was held in KTH. With a one week deadline, and a limited supply of parts, we planned and assembled a robot that was unrivaled by the other teams. The autonomous robot behavior was designed by me and I also proposed the design utilizing a plow and clever weight-distribution.
