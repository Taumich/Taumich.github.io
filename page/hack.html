OpenHack Coding for Humanity

OpenHack - Coding for Humanity - is a 48 hours long Hackathon where engineers and entrepreneurs gather to come up with different solutions and to leverage Open Source code to solve challenges society faces. OpenHack is backed by Engineers Without Borders and the participants competed in teams.

Out of 20 teams my team won the award for the best contribution in the category: Best Gamification. During the 48h hours we managed to create a prototype of a per to per platform for the Red Cross where people in need could connect to volunteers.

Motivation: "Simple, clean + catchy platform to engage participation. Easy to follow up on!"
